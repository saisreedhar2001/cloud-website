Dr.S.UdhayaKumar

Professor Head of the Department, Department of Cloud and Edge Analytics Institute of Computer Science and Engineering Saveetha School of Engineering

Dr.S.UDHAYAKUMAR has around 20 years of experience in teaching. Completed his Ph.D in the Domain of trusted cloud computing in 2017, with highly commendable reviews at B.S.Abdur Rahman University. Completed his Masters of Engineering, in Multimedia Technology in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at the prestigious College of Engineering, Anna University in 2003. Completed the Bachelor of Engineering in Electronic and communication at R.M.K. Engineering Collge, of University of Madras. He has been the Guest Faculty for Training Wipro Technologies Staffs, and conducted workshops in various institutions. He has published around 23 International Scopus Indexed Journals and more than 50 International Conference presentations across prestigious Universities of India. Has an h-index of 4, Reviewer for 3 Scopus indexed journals, and chaired 4 International conferences.
