Dr.D.Vinod

Associate Professor Department of Cloud and Edge Analytics Institute of Computer Science and Engineering Saveetha School of Engineering.

Dr. D.VINOD has around 16 years of experience in teaching and research in various Institutes under the department of Computer Science and Engineering. Completed his Ph.D. in the Domain of Information Security in the year 2018, with highly commendable reviews at Sathyabama Institute of Science and Technology. Completed his Masters of Engineering, in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at GKM Engineering College, Anna University in 2009. Completed the Bachelor of Technology in Information Technology in Adhiyamaan Engineering College, Anna University. He is currently acting as Advisor and Research Scientist in Ja-Assure Singapore. He has published around 16 International Scopus Indexed Journals and more than 25 International Conference presentations across prestigious Universities of India and other Countries. Has an h-index of 5, Reviewer for 3 Scopus indexed journals, and 1 Science indexed journal.
