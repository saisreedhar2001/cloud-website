Mr.R.Senthil Kumar

Assistant Professor Department of Cloud and Edge Analytics Institute of Computer Science and Engineering Saveetha School of Engineering

Mr.R. Senthil Kumar has completed his Masters in Computer Science and Engineering in Anna University. He has around 11 years of teaching experience in engineering colleges. Her areas of interest include Social Media Analytics, Text Analytics, Data Mining, and programming . He has published more than 10 research papers in various reputed and refereed journals. He has conducted programming Contest for students of Programming Club. He is presently pursuing his Ph.D under the able Guidance of Dr.SP. Chokkalingam, in the Institute of C.S.E.
